About

My name is Senku, and I am a 3D Modeler who specializes in Blockbench models.

In my work, I am inspired by video games and the endless possibilities of 3D modeling, and I strive to create stunning and immersive models that bring my ideas to life. I typically use Blockbench, a powerful and intuitive modeling tool, to create my designs, and I am always exploring new plugins and techniques to improve my workflow and push the boundaries of what is possible in 3D modeling. My notable projects include a series of character models for a Minecraft mod I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations, and I would love to hear from you if you have any questions or ideas about 3D modeling in Blockbench.
